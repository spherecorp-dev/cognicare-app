Hook B (Fear + Stat)

"95% of homemade pet diets are nutritionally incomplete. And the scariest part? Your pet looks perfectly fine until they're not."

Hook C (Question + Shock)

"Quick question — do you know your pet's zinc levels right now? Because 95% of pet parents who cook at home... don't. And that's a problem."

Hook D (IF/THEN — Halbert Classic)

"If you've ever Googled 'how to make homemade pet food'... then the next 15 minutes could save your pet from a nutritional deficiency you don't even know about."

Hook E (Controversy)

"The biggest lie in natural pet feeding? That you can figure out the right nutrition from a Google search and a Facebook group. You can't. Here's why."

Hook F (Social Proof + Curiosity)

"Thousands of pet parents just discovered something shocking about the food they've been making for their pets. The question is — will you find out too?"

Recomendação para Teste

Hook A vs Hook B (A = setup emocional longo, B = stat-driven rápido). Testar como pre-roll ad cortando nos primeiros 15 segundos.

Notas para o @copy-chief (Review)

1. Ângulo diferente da LP

A LP usa #2 (The $575 Secret) como primário com price anchoring. A VSL usa #3 (The Silent Deficiency) com narrativa emocional. São complementares — a LP converte via lógica/valor, a VSL converte via emoção/medo.

2. Demo section

Depende de mockup/wireframe do produto. No MVP, pode ser animação de telas. O script foi escrito de forma que funciona com screen recording real OU mockup animado.

3. Social proof ajustado
